About

I am a Brazilian UI|UX designer, with more than 10 years of experience working as a UI/UX Designer. Focused mainly on the user experience.

My goal is to give life and usability to the projects I work with.

My daily work is to transform what the final users need regarding concept and visual design. Before starting to create a high visual concept I prototype and test to validate the ideas as a whole.

I have graduated in Web Design, and I am currently working as an UX/UI designer in a leading edge School Management Software, in Ireland.

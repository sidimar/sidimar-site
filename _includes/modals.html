Event Case

The Miami Cocktail Challenge

It’s going to be another hot weekend in Miami and you’d like to organize a get- together with your friends to drink cocktails. You have a lot of friends with different schedules and varying tastes in bars, so it’s not so trivial to come to an agreement where as many friends as possible will come. But there’s an app for that. Or is there?

Job

Design a solution to solve the Miami Cocktail Challenge. You can design it as a website, mobile app or even wearable UI. You’re in charge, so you decide. At ysura we mainly use the iPad but it’s still up to you.

Deliverables

1. Set of wireframes explaining the solution user flow and interactions. Show us your design and interaction thinking.

2. At least one high-fi mockup of a screen. Feel free to build it in Photoshop, Illustrator or your design tool of choice. Show us your visual skills.

Solution

I asked some friends to been my "users" and I interviewed them to figure out how they understood it.

After that quick interview I start doing prototypes paper and asked them to try it and I could see if it's working right. At this point the idea was fail soon as possible and do it quick instead spend too much time.

I've used prototype paper where the prototypes was made using post it and pen to test with the users. It's very good to validate the ideas, improve, etc. Again quick and cheaper.
